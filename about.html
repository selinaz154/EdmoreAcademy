About Edmore

Edmore Academy, situated in Orange County, California, is a private educational institution working with a group of professional and experienced high school teachers and university professors educating grades 8–12 students. Among us are translators, writers, engineers, programmers, and full-time university and high school educators who share a deep passion for teaching.

We offer courses in AP, English writing, various levels of mathematics, and standardized test preparation classes, including TOEFL, IELTS, and ISEE. We also provide counseling services to both domestic and international students applying to universities and colleges.

Our academy has educated students across nine U.S. states — including New York, Colorado, and Texas — as well as international students all across the world.

Principal’s Welcome

Vivian Zhao, M.A. in English Literature, has over 20 years of teaching and educational leadership experience. Her philosophy centers on cultivating creativity, communication, and confidence in every learner. She believes education should empower students to discover their unique potential and pursue lifelong excellence.

Under Vivian’s leadership, Edmore Academy continues to expand partnerships with schools across California and beyond. Our students have successfully enrolled in top high schools and universities thanks to the dedication of our teachers and the supportive learning environment we foster.
