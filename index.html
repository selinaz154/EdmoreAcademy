About Us

Edmore Academy is a premier college prep and tutoring school based in California. We are committed to helping students strengthen their academic foundation, build confidence, and achieve success in competitive academic environments. Our instructors bring years of teaching experience and a passion for guiding students toward their academic and personal goals.

Our Programs

  • College Admissions Counseling: Personalized guidance for college applications, essays, and interviews.
  • Writing & English Enrichment: Creative and analytical writing skills for middle and high school students.
  • Mathematics & SAT/ACT Prep: Structured sessions to strengthen problem-solving and test performance.
  • AP & Honors Tutoring: Advanced subject tutoring in STEM and humanities courses.
